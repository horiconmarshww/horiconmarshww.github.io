Donate

We are seeking donations to help make our annual event the best possible. Our commitment is, and always has been, to ensure that all monies raised are put to good use for the direct benefit of the veterans.

Financial Donations

We are a non-profit organization (ID#45-3354139).
All contributions made are tax deductible. Donated monies are used first for food and beverage costs, as we are planning for at least 750 meals each year. Second, monies are used for our veteran's shells, licenses, and gas costs (gas cards).

Please mail donations to:

Horicon Marsh Wounded Warriors, Inc.
309 Mallard Court
Horicon, WI 53032

Items needed are food and beverages (list available upon request), serving items, items for our raffle fund raiser, gifts for our hero's gift bags, snack bag items to send while huntering, baked goods. Thank you for your generosity.
